DNA Double Helix Structure

⏱ Est. 25 min 🧩 Intermediate 🎯 High-frequency exam topic 🤖 AI-personalized for you

In 1953, Watson and Crick proposed the double helix model of DNA using X-ray crystallography data — one of the most significant scientific discoveries of the 20th century. It revealed the molecular basis for how genetic information is stored and transmitted across generations.

Memory tip
AT = 2 bonds, GC = 3 bonds

🔑 Chargaff's Rule: In any organism's DNA, the amount of Adenine (A) always equals Thymine (T), and Guanine (G) always equals Cytosine (C). That is, A = T and G = C.
